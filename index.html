THE MAGIC MICS

God does not play dice
-Albert Einstein

It was a long day on a hot summer's night, during a jamming session when Josh Schultz came up with the band name. The rest of the members fell on to the stage down from the heavens. Together they embarked on a journey to form the ultimate cover band, the best this world had ever seen. On a quest to find the magic microphone that would ultimately restore peace and order to the universe, the 5 lords of sound geared up, saddled up, and rode into the sunset with blistering tunes from bands such as The Beastie Boys, Poison, Tom Petty, GNR, and more. At the speed of light, around the world and back, there is no stopping them now. Only Satan himself would attempt, but with their mightly magical microphones, they would scream and blow Satan's eardrums right out of his eye sockets. This phenomenon happens only once every 10,000 years, blink and you shall miss it. Seize the chance to witness the spectacle, the sensational, sometimes odd, sometimes mad, but never a dull moment, the most magical of all mic's, The _____ _____.
